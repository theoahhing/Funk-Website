Funk's Produce

Funk's Produce was established in 2024 with a clear vision - to supply premium, high-quality produce with a level of service that sets a new standard.

What begun with one customer and a signle ute has rapidly grown into a trusted supplier across cafés, restaurants, schools, catering companies, retail outlets and even fellow wholesalers. This growth has been driven by a relentless focus on quality, consistency, and a commitment to delivering more than expected - the 1% that truly sets us apart.

Founded by a qualified chef, Funk's Produce is built on real industry experience and a deep understanding of what kitchens demand. Guided by the philosophy "Owned by a Chef, for chefs", we know that freshness, reliability, and precision are non-negotiable.

We work closely with growers and market agents to ensure a genuine paddock-to-plate approach, maintaining full visibility and control over quality from source to delivery. Our hands-on approach means we remain accessible, responsive, and committed to building strong, long-term relationships with every client.

At Funk's Prodduce, we don't just supply fruit and vegetables - we deliver a premium standard of produce, backed by service you can rely on.
